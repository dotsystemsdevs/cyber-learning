# Availability

![CIA Definitions](cia-definitions.png)

> The information is available to authorized users when needed.

---

## What it means
Availability means that systems and data are accessible when needed. If a system is taken down or made unreachable — availability is broken.

## Real examples
- A hospital's systems go offline during a ransomware attack
- A website is flooded with traffic and crashes
- A critical server fails with no backup

## Attacks that break Availability
- **DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)** — flood a system with traffic until it crashes
- **Ransomware** — encrypts all files, making them inaccessible
- **Physical destruction** — destroy hardware directly

## How it is protected
- Redundancy (backup systems that take over if one fails)
- DDoS protection (filter malicious traffic)
- Regular backups (restore data after an attack)
